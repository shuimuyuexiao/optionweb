Suggested email details

To understand the project faster, we recommend including the following in your first email:

  • The main business problem or project objective you want to solve.
  • The website, system, AI use case or maintenance scope you want to build.
  • Whether an existing site, system, server or relevant material already exists.
  • Your rough timeline, priorities and expected collaboration approach.

If the requirement is still unclear, a short business background and current challenge is enough to start.
